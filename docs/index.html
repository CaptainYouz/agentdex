Agentdex is fully local and fully private.

  • Runs 100% offline
    No internet connection required to scan, search, or edit.
  • No data collected by the app
    The desktop app has zero telemetry. Everything is 100% local.
  • No account, no cloud
    Nothing to sign up for. No backend. Nothing leaves your device.
  • Reads local files only
    Just the skill, agent, and rules files under your home directory.

The only optional network access is checking GitHub Releases for app updates — entirely under your control.
